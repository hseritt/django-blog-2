About Me

I am Harlin Seritt. I work at Formtek. Formtek is an Alfresco (the company) partner. I work mainly with Alfresco (the software) but also do some other work there with the following languages/platforms:

  • Python
  • Java
  • JavaScript
  • CSS
  • Ephesoft

I also set up environments for our QA testers:

  • AWS
  • Linux (RHEL & Ubuntu)
  • Weblogic
  • Windows servers with SAML integration (usually with Alfresco)

I am also proficient with the following:

  • Tomcat
  • Nginx and Apache web servers
  • RDBMS: MySQL, Postgresql, MS SQL, Oracle, IBM DB2
  • Solr

I got my start in the technology field relatively late. I didn't go to college until well after I got out of the military. I managed to get a CIS degree (AS) at Griffin Technical College in 2004 -- I was 35 years old. After that, things kind of took off from there. For my work history in this field, please have a look at my LinkedIn page.
